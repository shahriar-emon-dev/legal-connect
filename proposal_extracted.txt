1. Executive Summary

LegalConnect is a next-generation, web-based Freelance Legal Marketplace — purpose-built as the &quot;Upwork for Legal Professionals&quot; — that transforms how citizens and businesses in Bangladesh access legal expertise. Originally conceived as a Flutter-based mobile consultation app, this upgraded version evolves into a full-scale platform built with Next.js, Supabase, and TypeScript.



The platform creates a transparent, competitive, and structured digital bridge between legal professionals (lawyers, advocates, legal consultants) and clients (individuals, SMEs, corporations). Lawyers list their expertise across specialized departments; clients post legal gigs or cases with budget ranges; lawyers bid with proposals; and a structured Workspace manages the full case lifecycle — from contract creation to milestone tracking to secure document exchange.



Platform Tagline

&quot;Post your case. Choose your counsel. Track your justice.&quot; — LegalConnect democratizes access to legal services by bringing the freelance economy model to law.





2. Problem Statement

The legal services sector in Bangladesh remains largely undigitized, opaque, and inaccessible to a large segment of the population. The key pain points that LegalConnect is designed to solve are:


#
Problem
Impact
1
Opaque Legal Fees
Clients have no baseline for what a service should cost. Lawyers quote arbitrarily, creating distrust.
2
Inability to Find Niche Experts
Finding a specialist in IP law, arbitration, or cyber law requires personal networks — impossible for most people.
3
No Competitive Bidding System
Clients cannot compare proposals from multiple lawyers. They accept the first contact they make.
4
Inefficiency of Offline Paperwork
Legal documents are shared via WhatsApp or physical hand-offs, creating massive security and compliance risks.
5
Zero Case Transparency
Once engaged, clients have no structured way to track case progress, court dates, or milestones.
6
No Trust Infrastructure
No public rating, review, or credentials verification system exists for independent lawyers.
7
Geographic Barriers
Excellent lawyers in one district are invisible to clients in another.

4. Core Features — Full Upgrade




4.1  The Job Board (Legal Gig Marketplace)

The centerpiece feature that transforms LegalConnect from a directory into a true marketplace:


Clients post &quot;Legal Cases&quot; with title, department tag, description, budget range (BDT), and deadline


Cases are visible to all verified lawyers in the matched department


Lawyers submit &quot;Proposals&quot; including a cover letter, proposed fee, timeline, and relevant experience


Clients review proposals side-by-side and Accept, Decline, or Request More Info


Upon acceptance, a Workspace is automatically created for that contract




4.2  Smart Lawyer Profiles with Dynamic SEO


Every lawyer gets a unique, SEO-optimized URL: /lawyers/[slug] (e.g., /lawyers/sara-hossain-ip-law)


Profile includes: photo, bio, bar registration number, verified badge, department tags, years of experience


Public review scores, completed case count, response rate, and featured case studies


Next.js generateMetadata() creates individual Open Graph metadata for each lawyer page


Structured JSON-LD schema for Google lawyer/attorney rich results




4.3  Case Workspace &amp; Milestone Tracking


Each accepted contract creates a private Workspace visible only to the assigned lawyer and client


Case is broken into user-defined Milestones (e.g., Filing, Discovery, Hearing, Verdict, Appeal)


Each milestone has a status: Pending / In Progress / Completed / Blocked


Visual progress bar at workspace top showing overall case completion percentage


Court date calendar with automated reminders via Supabase Edge Functions + email




4.4  Secure Document Vault


All file uploads stored in Supabase Storage with bucket-level access control


Signed URL generation on each access request — links expire after 15 minutes


File categories: Evidence, Contracts, Court Orders, Personal ID, Correspondences


Version history: re-upload creates a new version, old versions archived not deleted


Audit log of every file access event (who viewed, when, IP address)




4.5  Real-Time Chat System


Built on Supabase Realtime Channels — no third-party service dependency


Each Workspace has its own dedicated chat thread (channel isolation by contract_id)


Lawyers can only message clients they have an active contract with (RLS enforced)


File sharing inline in chat with automatic upload to Document Vault


Read receipts, typing indicators, and timestamp display




4.6  Review &amp; Credibility System


Clients can leave a star rating (1–5) and detailed review after case closure


Lawyers can respond publicly to reviews (one response per review)


LegalConnect Verified Badge: awarded after credentials are checked by admin


Composite score shown publicly: weighted average of last 12 months of reviews


Flagging system for fake reviews — flagged reviews go to admin moderation queue




4.7  Admin Control Panel


Full CRUD on users, lawyers, departments, job listings


Lawyer verification workflow: review bar registration, NID, and credentials


Dispute resolution tools: freeze a workspace, flag a user, issue a warning


Platform analytics dashboard: active jobs, proposal conversion rate, revenue


Content management: update department descriptions, banners, and FAQ




4.8  Notification Engine


In-app notifications powered by Supabase Realtime (notification bell with unread count)


Email notifications via Resend API triggered by Supabase Edge Functions


Push notifications for: new proposals received, bid accepted, milestone updated, new message


Weekly digest email: case status summary for active clients

5.3  Security — Row Level Security (RLS) Policies

Supabase RLS is the cornerstone of client-attorney privilege on the platform. Every sensitive table has policies that enforce data isolation at the database level — no amount of API manipulation can bypass these:


Table
RLS Policy
Effect
messages
contract_id must belong to authenticated user as client OR lawyer
A lawyer cannot read another lawyer&apos;s client messages
documents
contract_id must belong to authenticated user + is_verified must be true for lawyer
Unverified lawyers cannot access any client files
contracts
Only client_id and lawyer_id of that contract can SELECT
Contracts are fully private to participants
proposals
Lawyers can only see their own proposals; clients see proposals for their jobs
Prevents price collusion between competing lawyers
milestones
Read/write restricted to contract participants only
Case progress is invisible to non-participants
reviews
Clients can INSERT once per contract; lawyers can UPDATE only own response field
Prevents fake review flooding




5.4  Serverless Logic — Next.js Server Actions

Business-critical logic runs in Next.js Server Actions (server-only code with no client exposure):


submitProposal(): Validates lawyer is verified, job is still open, no duplicate proposal exists


acceptProposal(): Marks proposal as accepted, closes job, creates contract row atomically


generateSignedUrl(): Creates time-limited (15-min) Supabase Storage URLs for document access


closeCaseAndTriggerReview(): Marks contract complete, sends review request email via Resend


flagReview(): Marks review as flagged, creates admin notification for moderation

7. Monetization Strategy

LegalConnect employs a multi-stream revenue model appropriate for a freelance marketplace:


Revenue Stream
Model
Rate / Detail
Service Commission
Platform fee deducted from lawyer&apos;s payment on contract completion
10% of agreed contract value (standard industry rate for legal platforms)

Monthly SaaS plan for lawyers with premium features
Free: 3 proposals/month | Pro (BDT 999/mo): unlimited bids + analytics
Featured Profile Placement
Lawyers pay to appear at the top of department search results
BDT 1,500–3,000 / 30-day campaign, max 3 featured per department
Verified Fast-Track
Expedited credential verification for lawyers (standard is 5–7 days)
BDT 500 one-time fee for 24-hour verification turnaround
Enterprise Client Accounts
Corporate clients (law firms, companies) get a dedicated team dashboard
Custom pricing, negotiated annual contracts



Revenue Projection Note

At 100 completed contracts/month averaging BDT 10,000 each, the 10% commission alone yields BDT 100,000/month. This scales linearly with platform growth. Subscription revenue provides predictable MRR independent of transaction volume.

9. Conclusion

LegalConnect in its upgraded form is not merely a mobile app — it is a complete digital ecosystem for legal services in Bangladesh. By adopting the proven freelance marketplace model (used globally by platforms like Upwork and Toptal) and applying it to the legal domain, the platform addresses a significant market gap.



The combination of Next.js for performance and SEO, Supabase for real-time capabilities and security, and a carefully designed freelance workflow makes LegalConnect technically robust and commercially viable. The departmental structure, milestone-based case tracking, secure document vault, and competitive bidding system collectively deliver a product that is simultaneously more transparent, more accessible, and more trustworthy than any existing legal service option in Bangladesh.



This upgrade represents the evolution from a student project to a startup-grade product — a demonstration of full-stack engineering competency, product thinking, and real-world impact design.
